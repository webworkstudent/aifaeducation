About us

COMPANY PROFILE

We introduce ourselves one of the fastest upcoming national educational brand in India in the Name of AIFA “Asian Institute of Finance & Accounts”. It is a cluster of eminent, focused, innovative and experienced professionals and educationists with a common goal to share their core knowledge and experience to create competent service force acceptable and employable in the corporate world. Keeping the same vision, a very new era of education has born in “YEAR 2012”. Our prime focus areas are the two sectors like Accounts & Finance & IT. The main object of developing these courses/training is to inculcate technical & employable knowledge which are very helpful for career advancement. We are looking forward on software division, government projects, Placement Portal and many more, as we strongly believe that knowledge/ experience is flow of innovative thoughts and ideas, if is complied, edited and improved on keeping corporate need then it be a blessing for the mankind forever.

SPECIAL FEATURES

  • Class will be delivered by expert training faculties.
  • One to one practice facilities is available for better understanding of deliverables.
  • Practice texts & adequate exam pattern to evaluate proper knowledge.
  • Projects/Case studies /workshop/revision classes for creating sound knowledge.
  • Adequate placement support for deserving students.
  • On phone support facility for students to resolve working hassles.
  • On job training programme.
  • 100% Practical Oriented Training.

VISION

Our is to be the best in the field of education and in creation of professionals who will lead country in future through immense value. As we strongly believe that “The Gracious humanitarian Act is giving excellent contribution to training and developing Human Recourses, leading them to having good morals and virtues of high quality to continue constructing and developing the Country”.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide quality education & training at affordable limits to meet the demands of Cut-throat challenge of industry at Global Standard.
