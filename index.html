About us

AIFA, a corporate focused skill development company with close to 12 years’ experience in Skill Development training. AIFA is the training partner of National Skills Development Corporation (NSDC) & Telecom Sector (Skill Council TSSC) upholding the Indian government’s vision, the company is committed to create a ‘skilled India’ through an array of innovative workforce solutions.
